Lewis and Clark Expedition

This website is intended to give an introduction to the journey of Lewis and Clark into the territory gained through the Louisiana Purchase.

The menu in the upper right corner has links to the descriptions of some key members on the expedition and a Scrollytelling of the journey in a day-by-day breakdown with records of all journal entries.
